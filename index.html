About me

Shushil is a Structural Engineer and Engineering Application Developer for BIM and Design Automation. With his eagerness for acquiring new skills and proven quick learning abilities he is thriving in developing impactful and innovative automation solutions for multiple disciplines to bring out efficiency; both in terms of time and cost of project. He is a proactive self-learner and can easily adapt to modern design tools and is always open for collaborating with various stakeholders to bring out high quality work.

In the past, he has worked as a Structural Design Engineer where he gained decent fluency in performing Structural Analysis and Design of residential building projects and with zeal of automation from within, he standardized a system for report generation process and speed up the output by almost three times. He then quickly graduated to become a full-time automation engineer to develop multiple BIM automation tools for clients like: UK Highways Team, Noida International Airport and more at Jacobs. In him, one can find the mindset of a leader who is always in hunt for next challenge and work together as a team player to achieve common goal that makes a positive impact on the society.
